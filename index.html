This app is prepared by Mobile Development Group, IIT Roorkee and is meant for everyone in IITR, be it students or faculty. We always crib about the conditions in IITR not upto the mark. Report your grievances to us, let it be of any magnitude, be it potholes in road, waterlogging or unhygienic conditions.
